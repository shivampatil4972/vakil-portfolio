About Me

Passionate About Justice

I am Adv.Ashwanith Chelekar(Nana), a Senior Advocate enrolled at the Bombay High Court with over 15 years of distinguished legal practice. My journey in law began with a firm belief that every individual deserves quality legal representation, regardless of the complexity of their situation.

Specializing in Criminal Defence, Civil Litigation, Family Law, and Corporate Advisory, I have represented clients across all tiers of the judiciary — from District Courts to the Supreme Court of India. My approach combines rigorous legal analysis with a deep understanding of the human element behind every case.

FAQs

Common Questions

Answers to the questions most clients ask before their first consultation.

What are the consultation fees?
Initial consultation fees start at ₹500 for a 30-minute session. This allows me to understand your case and provide initial guidance. Fees for full representation vary depending on the complexity of the matter and will be discussed transparently during the first meeting. There are no hidden charges.
How do I book an appointment?
You can book an appointment through three easy ways: (1) Fill out the consultation form on this page, (2) Call us directly at +91 98608 20057 during office hours (Mon–Sat, 9:30 AM – 6:30 PM), or (3) Click the WhatsApp button for immediate assistance. Emergency legal matters are handled on a priority basis.
What documents should I bring for my first meeting?
For your first consultation, please bring: Government-issued ID proof (Aadhaar/PAN), any court notices or legal documents you have received, relevant agreements or contracts, photographs or evidence related to your case, and any previous legal correspondence. Don't worry if you don't have everything — we can guide you on document collection.
Do you offer online consultations?
Yes! We offer online video consultations via Zoom or Google Meet for clients in other cities or countries. NRI clients and those with mobility constraints particularly benefit from this service. Please mention your preference for online consultation when booking your appointment.
How long does a legal case typically take?
Case timelines vary significantly. Simple matters like bail applications may be resolved in days; consumer forum cases in 3–12 months; civil property cases in 1–3 years; and complex criminal cases may take 2–5 years depending on court schedule and appeal stages. I provide realistic timelines based on your specific case and keep you updated at every stage.
